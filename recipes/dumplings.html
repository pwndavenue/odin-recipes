Dumplings

Photo taken from https://www.flickr.com/photos/155388070@N07/43303533072

Simple and short dumplings recipe for everyone

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter or margarine
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup milk

Recipe

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes.

Enjoy!
